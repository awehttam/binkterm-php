Solitaire

How to Play

  • Click the deck to draw cards (toggle Draw 3 for faster play).
  • Click a face-up card (or a stack in tableau) to select it.
  • Click a tableau column or foundation to move the selection (if legal).
  • Tableau builds down in rank, alternating colors.
  • Foundations build up by suit from Ace to King.
  • Use Undo if you misclick.
